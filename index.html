Data is the most valuable resource in the world. Ocean Exchange will unlock it.

Ocean Exchange is a global, decentralized data marketplace to make data available for everyone. Ocean Exchange allows data providers to find data consumers, with trust, security, compliance and transparency.

The platform offers a secure, public and decentralized infrastructure with built in pricing mechanisms and smart contracts for sharing of data in accordance with your level of comfort, privacy and data governance regulations.

Ocean Exchange allows data to be tokenized and sold, rewarding data providers for extracting, refining and publishing data onto the exchange.

Powered by IPBD & BigchainDB

Ocean Exchange uses IPDB, a global decentralized database for the planet that allows metadata to be stored in an immutable, public database, while giving the owner full control over their data.

IPDB is powered by BigchainDB, one of the pioneering blockchain technologies in the space.
